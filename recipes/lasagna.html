Description

This is a recipe for the most general lasagna you can find

ingredients

  • frozen lasagna pasta
  • pasta sauce
  • cheese
  • meaaaat (fake or real)

steps

  1. Preheat oven to 800F
  2. Put a layer of meat, cheese, sauce, then pasta noodles into a pan,
  3. Repeat until you get to the top of the pan
  4. When oven is preheated, place the pan in the oven
  5. Take the pan out when your heart desires.
